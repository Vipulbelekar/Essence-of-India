Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan, is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Located in Agra, this stunning marble mausoleum was constructed between 1632 and 1653 as a tribute to his wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

It is an architectural masterpiece, with intricate carvings, a massive dome, and beautiful gardens surrounding it.

Red Fort

The Red Fort is a historic fort in Delhi, constructed by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in 1648. It served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years.

The fort is famous for its massive red sandstone walls, beautiful gardens, and the annual Independence Day celebrations where the Indian Prime Minister hoists the national flag.
